The Different Zones

Toggle through Zone 1, Zone 2 & Zone 3 below.

Zone 1 is defined as the circle, the radius of which is twice the distance between the center of optic disc and center of macula. Zone 2 is defined as the area from the edge of the zone 1 peripherally to a point tangential to the nasal ora serrata. Zone 3 is the residual temporal crescent of retina anterior to zone 2.
